Your Journey

Five extraordinary South Pacific destinations, each offering a unique blend of turquoise lagoons, vibrant reefs, and rich Polynesian culture. This guide compares flights, resorts, activities, and dining for a family of six — two adults and four children (ages 12, 10, 8, and 4) — travelling business class from San Francisco.

Recommendations

Best routing: Fiji — Nonstop A350, 11 hours. Most family-friendly with kids' clubs at every resort.

Best lagoon: Aitutaki — Ranked #1 in the world. Also lowest airfare at $5,411/pp.

Best French Polynesia: Fakarava or Rangiroa — Same 787 nonstop to Papeete. Raw & unspoiled vs. more infrastructure.

Best adventure: Samoa — To Sua, sliding rocks, fire dancing. Combines well with a Fiji stopover.
